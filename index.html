Why Choose New Druid Institute?

Meet the Owner

Jeremy Weber

Jeremy grew up in the woods and fields of Indiana. He holds a B.S. in Forestry and Natural Resources and an M.S. in Botany and Plant Pathology, both from Purdue University.

Over the past 20+ years, Jeremy has worked for two ecological restoration companies, been a climbing arborist and tree crew leader, served universities in Indiana and Montana, managed resources with the Indiana Department of Natural Resources, and overseen parks departments. He's also worked as a field supervisor, manager/administrator, prescribed burn boss, invasive species surveyor, corporate safety and operations trainer, educator, and consulting forester throughout the Midwest and Northern Rockies. He has served on many boards and committees over the years.

Jeremy’s adventurous spirit has taken him across 14 countries, 43 states, and all 92 counties of Indiana — though his wife and kids tolerate his spirit reasonably well. His interests include outdoor activities, physics, chemistry, and science fiction, blending a nerdy knowledge base with humor shaped by influences like Kurt Vonnegut, Looney Tunes, and Calvin & Hobbes.
