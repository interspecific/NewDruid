Welcome to the New Druid Institute

Learn About the Natural World, Safety, and Some Ancient Ways

Why Choose New Druid Institute?

  • Reduce Risk: Safer and more efficient outdoor work through hazard recognition and mitigation training.
  • Connect with Nature: Rebuild awareness of local plants, natural cycles, and the healing power of nature.
  • Improve Operations: Boost efficiency, leadership, and field techniques through professional training.
  • Science-backed Training: Practical, peer-reviewed, and field-proven methods for sustainable living and working.

Training Programs

  • Safe Chainsaw Use and Tree Felling
  • American Red Cross First Aid/CPR/AED Certification
  • Materials Handling Equipment Training
  • Operations Observation and Consulting

Meet the Owner

Jeremy Weber holds degrees in Forestry, Botany, and Plant Pathology from Purdue University. He brings over 20 years of experience in restoration, forestry, safety training, and resource management across the Midwest and Northern Rockies. Jeremy is a certified Forest Therapy Practitioner, blending modern science with ancient wisdom to help people reconnect with the land.
