Our Services

New Druid Institute offers a broad range of ecological, land stewardship, and sustainable living services. Each service is tailored to meet the specific needs of individuals, businesses, organizations, and agencies.

Prescribed Burns

Fire is an essential ecological tool for maintaining healthy prairies, forests, and wetlands. We offer planning, permitting support, and implementation of prescribed burns with safety and ecological integrity at the forefront.

Ecological Restoration Plans

We design restoration plans for native habitats, including prairie, wetland, and woodland ecosystems. Plans are science-driven and include site assessment, species recommendations, invasive species control strategies, and long-term management guidelines.

Forestry Consulting

Our forestry consulting services include forest stewardship plans, timber stand improvement guidance, habitat enhancement recommendations, invasive species assessments, and sustainable forest management strategies.

Garden Consulting

Whether you're building a pollinator garden, a native habitat planting, or a backyard edible garden, we provide consulting on design, species selection, soil health, and management to ensure sustainable, beautiful results.

Permaculture Design

Our permaculture services integrate agriculture, landscaping, and ecological design to create productive, resilient systems that benefit both people and nature. Designs are tailored to your land, goals, and local ecosystem.

Landscape Design

We offer environmentally conscious landscape designs for homes, businesses, and community spaces. Emphasis is placed on using native plants, minimizing water use, and creating wildlife-friendly environments.

Drone Photography

We provide aerial imagery for site assessments, documentation, marketing, and restoration project monitoring. High-resolution images and videos are available for landowners, businesses, and organizations.

Site Assessments

We perform detailed site assessments to evaluate ecological conditions, identify opportunities and challenges, and provide tailored recommendations for land management, restoration, or sustainable development.

Rain Garden Installation

Rain gardens help capture and filter stormwater while providing beautiful habitat for pollinators. We offer site-specific designs, species recommendations, and full installation services.

Plant ID Workshops

Our workshops teach participants how to identify native plants, understand plant communities, and apply plant knowledge to gardening, foraging, restoration, and land stewardship efforts. Workshops are seasonal and can be customized for groups or individuals.
