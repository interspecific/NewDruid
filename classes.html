Explore the Natural World with Us

Below are our most common courses and services to help you expand your knowledge and skills. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, our courses offer something for everyone.

Red Cross Adult First Aid/CPR/AED Certification Course

The American Red Cross offers top-tier emergency response curricula. Students who successfully complete this course will receive an official Red Cross certification. This is an in-person class with both lecture and realistic, hands-on skills practice and assessments.

Contact us for more information. To register for the course, click here.

Chainsaw Safety and Tree Felling

Learn chainsaw safety and proper tree felling techniques from an instructor with over 20 years of professional experience. This class includes 5–6 hours of classroom instruction and 5–8 hours of field practice with demonstrations and assessments.

Click here for more information and here to register.

Materials Handling Equipment Safety

Get trained on the safe operation of skidsteers, mini excavators, and other equipment. This two-day course covers classroom learning followed by hands-on skills assessments tailored to the attachments you plan to use.

Tree and Plant Identification / Basic Ecology

Learn how to identify plants, understand plant communities, and read the natural signs around you. Seasonal courses may also cover foraging techniques and traditional medicinal uses of native species.
